Juicy Roasted Chicken

It's my grandmother's recipe

She is German and she used to do it this way all the time

I never have had a chicken this juicy before. With this little trick, people's going to eat in silence

Ingredients for 6 servings

  • 1 whole chicken, giblets removed
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1/2 cup margarine, divided
  • 1 stalk celery, leaves removed
  • 1 teaspoon of honey if you want

Please add all ingredients to shopping list

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F ( 175 degrees C
  2. Place chicken in a roasting pan and season generously inside and out with salt and pepper. Sprinkle inside and out with onion powder. Places 3 tablespoons margarine in the chicken cavity. Arrange dollops of the remainning margarine around the chicken's exterior. Cut the celery into 3 or 4 pieces and place in the chickent cavity
  3. Bake uncoverd 1 hour and 15 minutes in the preheated ovento a minimum internal temperrature of 180 degrees F ( 82 degrees C. Remove from heat and baste with melted margarine and drippings. Cover with aluminum foil and allow to rest about 30 minutes before serving
